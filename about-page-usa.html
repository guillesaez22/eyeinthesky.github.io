About Eye in the Sky Media

Eye in the Sky Media’s story is quite simple! Kevin and Stephen became great friends when they both worked for the same company a few years ago. Kevin picked up his passion for UAS/Drone operation in December of 2019 and began sending videos and photos he produced with his drone to Stephen. Stephen, with his love for aviation, was taken aback that a drone could shoot such high quality video and photos. He thought to himself, “This looks awesome, and it’s a way for me to get back into aviation!” He picked up his drone not long after and began learning to fly and shoot amazing photos and videos. Before long, the idea to go professional with their passion for drone flight was born. Eye in the Sky Media is North Carolina’s premier aerial photography company. Together, Kevin and Stephen combine their professional skills to produce world-class aerial photography.
